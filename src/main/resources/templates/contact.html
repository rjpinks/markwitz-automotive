About Us

Here at Markwitz

We here at Markwitz Automotive are passionate about vehicles. Beyond that we're passionate about people. So it only makes sense that we're great at pairing people with the right used car. Our salesman have been serving our area for the last 40 years, and we're eager to continue!

I, Mark Markwitz, began selling used cars after me and my son worked on his first car together. Before we knew it, we were buying project cars and flipping them after we were done. After he went off to college, I didn't stop. Now, we have some of the best mechanics, employees, and cars in our area!
